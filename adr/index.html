Lifecycle rules

  • ADRs are never silently rewritten to erase history.
  • A changed decision creates a new ADR that supersedes the old one and links both directions.
  • Proposed means direction, not implemented runtime behavior.
  • Accepted decisions still require evidence links and review triggers.
  • Rejected and superseded records remain discoverable.
